From Zack

I am so excited that you are looking at our website. That probably means you are someone we like. Something I'm even more excited about is to marry Kristen. We met one dark night in our favorite Whiskey bar. It was here that I won her over with my tales of cooking.

It was only a short time later when she looked at me and said, "I think you should know, I accepted a fellowship in Orlando." I didn't know what to say, I was lost for works. I thought to myself, "What's a fellowship?"

Time zoomed on and I found myself in Florida standing 3 miles from a spaceship having a serious thought, "If this spaceship blows up should I try to pull her under this bleacher or just shield her with my body. She would be really valuable in the aftermath".

Something like 6 months and 40 hours later we arrived in the extra smokey mountains of the Pacific North West.

Life is weird.

From Kristen

Many of you know Zack and I have been committed to each other for the past two years. Our meet cute included a long walk with a different man, a beer tent sign, two cocktails and discovering the definition of sapiophile. Several days later, our very first date ended with discussing deal breakers…”Not believing in science”...I am certain it did not take Zack long to figure out I did not have any issues with that requirement. If anything, he should have been concerned about my “left-brained” tendencies.

The decade prior to meeting Zack, I have worked very hard at balancing the left and right brain dominance. Many of you reading this website have played a VERY important role in developing my emotional intelligence and I am forever grateful to you. What I struggled the most with during this time was the brain and heart connection.

Zack has many traits and qualities that make him an amazing life partner; great listener, compassionate, an amazing chef and mixologist, family and friend focused, world traveler. All things that I discovered on our very first date that lingered into the late hours. Ultimately, PATIENCE is Zack’s #1 quality that I am most appreciative of.

I want to thank you for all your love and support that you have given me. To Zack’s friends and family, thank you for influencing his character that I heavily rely upon. All of you mean so much to both of us and we look forward to continuing to include you in our life’s journey.
