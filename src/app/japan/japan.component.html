Carbon Offset Incentive

Offset your personal CO2 emissions while earning Beam

Beam's carbon offset system works like a rewards program - the more offsets you buy, the more BEAM Tokens you can earn- tokens that can be freely used across the platform to invest. You'll be able to track your earnings and redemptions on your user dashboard.

Token Details

Beam is powered by ethereum-based smart tokens, or BEAM Tokens, which act as carriers for equity crowdfunding 'smart contracts'.

To invest in a startup, users make a contribution in BEAM as a way to interact with the smart contract for the crowdfund. By doing so, Beam ensures your contribution is only made if the crowdfund is successful, and is tracked as part of the startup's community of investors - no matter where you are or what amount you contribute.

In short, Beam tokens ensure that user funds are invested automatically and transparently. For more on our tokens, see our white paper.

Sale Details

To kickstart the crowdfunding platform, Beam will hold a token sale, allowing individuals to purchase Beam Tokens during the sale in exchange for Ether (ETH). Sign up above for email updates for more information on the Token sale.
